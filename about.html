who we are?

Velammal College of Engineering & Technology, Madurai, has an independent Training & placement Cell devoted to cater the needs of the organizations in conducting campus interviews for placements. It is headed by Mr. P. Srinivasaperumal, and is assisted by Staff and Students Placement coordinators who lead a team of placement representatives from various courses of study. The TPO Cell plays a very important and key role in counseling and guiding the students for their successful career a placement which is a crucial interface between the stages of completion of academic program of the students and their entry into the Corporates. The cell also coordinates various activities to the career of the students along with the industrial training.


Placement procedure

  1. Companies are invited to the college by the TPO Cell.
  2. The companies are also invited to contact the college placement officer for their requirement.
  3. To the responding companies, the details of the final year students opting for placement are sent as per their requirements.
  4. The company is assigned a convenient date.
  5. After confirmation by the company, a pre-placment talk is arranged on the date.
  6. The company condcuts their recruitment program in VCET campus.
  7. The selected candidates are intimated of their recruitment status on the same day.
